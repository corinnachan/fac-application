Why I'd like to join the Summer 2024 FAC Cohort

I am hugely passionate about encouraging more women and under-represented groups to become developers, and aspire to be a role model for those who join this career from non-traditional paths after I do so myself. After many years of working adjacently to developers in my career and teaching myself to code in a hobbyist capacity, I am keen to build a foundation where I could realistically pursure developer roles in a professional capacity.
